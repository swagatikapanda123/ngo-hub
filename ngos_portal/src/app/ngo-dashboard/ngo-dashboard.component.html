NGO DASHBOARD

Welcome to NGO HUB, We gather NGO information under one portal that will serve the people in time of adversity.

We intend to address a real-world issue by establishing a platform that will help public getting NGOs information at an instant when needed. It has been realised that residents of one city are unaware of local non-governmental organisations (NGOs) that can help them in an emergency. Accessing the appropriate NGO or making any form of contact is also difficult, and as a result, many people are unable to receive assistance, prompting us to create something that would connect NGOs with users.
