About Us

Welcome to NGO HUB, We gather NGO information under one portal that will serve the people in time of adversity.

We intend to address a real-world issue by establishing a platform that will help public getting NGOs information at an instant when needed. It has been realised that residents of one city are unaware of local non-governmental organisations (NGOs) that can help them in an emergency. Accessing the appropriate NGO or making any form of contact is also difficult, and as a result, many people are unable to receive assistance, prompting us to create something that would connect NGOs with users. This is a two-way platform where non-profits can sign up and gain access to the features in their own dashboard. The pandemic has highlighted the importance of local non-governmental organisations (NGOs) in saving millions of lives, and in light of that impact, we are attempting to bridge the gap between people in need and organisations that can assist.

OUR STORY

During the pandemic, we saw the role of individuals or organisations who voluntarily stepped forward to assist people who were going through a difficult time. The fact that many people were seen struggling to arrange blood or any other necessity for their family members was extremely painful, and many people still appear to struggle with this as they do not have a potential source who could assist them. This was the sole reason for creating such a platform.

OUR DREAM

Our goal is to have as many NGOs as possible registered on our portal. We intend to make all registered NGO contact information available to the public so that people can easily collaborate or communicate in times of need.

The major goal was to develop a special and practical "NGO PORTAL" that stands out from other applications by offering superior quality and service. The proposed approach is intended to make it easier to find prospective assistance in an emergency.

Together, we can transform our society into one that is more sustainable. Numerous NGOs step up freely to address social issues, but by recognizing those striving to improve living standards, advance society, and safeguard the environment, we may also join the team and contribute to a worthwhile cause. In a similar vein, we can readily contact NGOs when we need assistance.
